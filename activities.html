June 10-12: Boston, MA

Tuesday, June 10

  • Arrive in Boston at 5:30 PM
  • Louisa May Alcott's Orchard House: 399 Lexington Rd, Concord, MA (20 min from hotel), family rate $12 & $6.40
  • Reference: Boston 2-Day Itinerary, Sightseeing Passes

Wednesday, June 11

  • Hop on Hop off Trolley: 2-day ticket, $82
  • Freedom Trail: $17.85, Walk into History Tour (free, some optional tours have fees), park at Boston Common, take T and yellow bus to Bunker Hill, work backwards, split into two mornings if hot, favorites: USS Constitution, Bunker Hill, Quincy Market, Old North Church
  • Paul Revere's House: $6
  • Boston Tea Party Ships & Museum: Cost not specified
  • Boston Children's Museum: $26
  • USS Constitution Museum: $15
  • Cape Cod Canal Cruise: $21
  • Harvard/MIT: 255 Main St, Cambridge, MA, free (MIT tour $22)
  • Piers Park: Near airport, free, overlook boats and downtown Boston
  • Mayflower II: Plymouth (1h away), might not visit
  • Plymouth Rock: No cost specified
  • Lexington Green National Historic Site: No cost specified
  • Minute Man Visitor Center: Open until 5 PM, shows movie about American Revolution

Thursday, June 12

  • Optional last Boston attraction before driving to Stowe, VT

June 12: Stowe, VT

  • Joseph Smith Birthplace: 357 LDS Ln, South Royalton, VT (2h 15m from Boston), M-Sat 8 AM-5 PM, Sun 1 PM-5 PM, Info
  • Cold Hollow Cider Mill: 3600 Waterbury-Stowe Rd, Waterbury Center, VT (55 min from Joseph Smith Birthplace), 8 AM-6 PM daily (closed Thanksgiving & Christmas), old-fashioned cider press, apple cider donuts, cafe, vintage mill & gift shop, Info

June 13-14: Susquehanna, PA

Friday, June 13

  • Priesthood Restoration Site: 27832 Route 171, Oakland Township, PA, 10 AM-7 PM (M-Sat), 1 PM-5 PM (Sun), visit if time permits
  • Fort William Henry Museum: Optional, cost not specified

Saturday, June 14

  • Priesthood Restoration Site & Joseph and Emma's Home: 27832 Route 171, Oakland Township, PA, 10 AM-7 PM, includes visitor’s center, cemetery, Emma’s childhood home, Susquehanna River

June 14-16: Palmyra, NY

Saturday, June 14

  • Priesthood Restoration Site: Visit at 10 AM before driving to Palmyra
  • Watkins Glen State Park: 1009 N Franklin, Watkins Glen, NY, $8 parking, gorge trail with 19 waterfalls, en route to Palmyra
  • Whitmer Farm: 1451 Aunkst Rd, Waterloo, NY, 10 AM-7 PM (M-Sat), 1 PM-5 PM (Sun, maybe 7 PM), where the church was organized, 30 min from Watkins Glen
  • Grandin Printing Press: 217 E Main, Palmyra, NY, 10 AM-7 PM, where Book of Mormon was printed
  • Martin and Lucy Harris Farm: 2905 Maple Ave, Palmyra, NY, 10 AM-7 PM, no interior tours, walk around outside, visit on 6/14 or 6/15

Sunday, June 15

  • Attend local Sacrament meeting
  • Sacred Grove: 843 Stafford Rd, Palmyra, NY, 10 AM-7 PM (M-Sat), 1 PM-5 PM (Sun, maybe 7 PM), take bug spray, jackets, journals, many benches
  • Joseph Smith Family Farm: 843 Stafford Rd, Palmyra, NY, 10 AM-7 PM (M-Sat), 1 PM-5 PM (Sun, maybe 7 PM), includes log home, frame home, barn, Cooper Shop, Welcome Center, Palmyra Temple
  • Hill Cumorah: 603 State Route 21, Palmyra, NY, 10 AM-7 PM (M-Sat), 1 PM-5 PM (Sun, maybe 7 PM), back route available, video of Hill Cumorah Pageant

Monday, June 16

  • Niagara Falls: 1h 40m from Sacred Grove, always open, $10-$20 parking, Maid of the Mist boat ride, skywalk, Visitor’s Center, museum
